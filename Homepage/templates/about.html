Designed for users and business owners alike

CollabFood help users decide on what to eat based on their likings. Users might be recommended food that they had not tried before but yet are suited to their taste buds as we use collaborative filtering to recommend users based on what similar users have tried and liked.

CollabFood provides business owners with useful data as insights for them to fine tune their business model in order to gain more revenue. Additionally, we also help promote their businesses to our users.
